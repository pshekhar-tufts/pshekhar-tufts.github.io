Prashant Shekhar

Postdoctoral fellow

Tufts University

Biography

Hi, I’m Prashant. Currently I am a postdoc at Tufts University’s Data Intensive Studies Center (DISC), working with Dr. Abani Patra . Currently, my work mainly focusses on learning hierarchical sparse representations for large datasets enabling faster inference. I am interested in using machine learning and physics to understand evolution of large cryospheric systems like polar icesheets.
